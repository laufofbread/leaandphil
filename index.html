Lea & Phil are Getting Married. 27th December 2013

The Plan

The wedding ceremony will begin at 4pm, and we would be grateful if guests could arrive at the church by 3.40pm. Parking is available on the road leading up to the church gates.

For those staying at Close House you are able to drop your bags off at the hotel before the ceremony and we have asked that as many rooms as possible are made available before the church service.

Having tied the knot, guests are invited to Close House for a drinks reception and dinner before retiring to the lounge where the log fire will be burning and the merriment can continue until the midnight hour.

For those guests staying with us at Close House we would be delighted if you are able to join us for breakfast the following morning.

Gifts

Your presence, not presents is all that we require, but if you do wish to buy us something, a contribution to our honeymoon would be appreciated.

Dress Code

Black tie and evening wear.

The Church

The Rvd. Bill Rigby would like to join us in welcoming you to the beautiful St. Peter's church for our special celebration.

As snowmen are a familiar sight in Bywell, and the church is down a small country lane, guests may wish to wrap up warm and ladies bring suitable additional footwear in case of snowfall.

Directions from A69 Westbound

Having joined the A69 travel for approximately 10 minutes (from Newcastle), then turn left when signposted for Stocksfield A6309; follow the road for approximately 5 minutes. You will eventually reach a cross junction (if you reach a bridge you have gone too far). At this junction go straight ahead for Bywell. Follow the road to the end where you will reach St Peter's Church.

Directions from A69 Eastbound

Travelling on the A69 East bound you will reach a roundabout signposted for Corbridge. Take the 3rd exit signposted for A68 Darlington. At the next roundabout take the first exit signposted for Stocksfield. Travel along the A695 for 5 minutes until you reach an S-bend signposted Stocksfield. Take the first left signposted Bywell and follow the road over a bridge bearing to the left until you reach a cross junction. Take an immediate left and follow the road to the end where you will reach St Peter's Church.

Directions to Close House

From St Peter’s Church drive to the end of the country lane and turn right. Travel approximately 500 yards and turn left before the bridge, signposted Ovingham/Wylam. Follow the road until you reach a T junction and turn right, signposted Ovingham/Wylam. Follow the road through the village signposted Wylam, eventually reaching Wylam where you should follow signposts for the A69. At the next junction turn right, signposted Heddon on the Wall. You will soon see a sign for Close House. Ignore this sign and continue driving until you reach the main entrance gates for Close House on your right.
